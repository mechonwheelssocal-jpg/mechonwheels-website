The Business

Skilled Troubleshooting Delivered to Your Door

Mech on Wheels is an independent mobile mechanic service built around accurate diagnostics, quality workmanship, and transparent communication.

We know the frustration of being overcharged or dealing with "parts swappers." Our approach is diagnostic-first, emphasizing OEM-minded procedures whenever possible. From simple maintenance to deeper drivability and no-start concerns, we provide clear documentation of findings and honest recommendations before any wrench is turned.

Skip the tow truck and the waiting room. We bring professional capability and real answers directly to your vehicle's location.
